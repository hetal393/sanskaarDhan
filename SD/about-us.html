What is Sanskaar Dhan

Sanskaar Dhan – An online kids paathshaala based on the path followed at Agas Ashram based on the teachings of Shrimad Rajchandraji.

A deep enrooted tree with Sanskaar is a shelf to its Soul withstanding all thicks and thins. Being like a canopy to the society and paving its way to eternal happiness. And a tree planted without Sanskaar is a hurricane to the society, Family and Ownselves
